Building infrastructure for agent-driven systems.

Aithernet is a platform for secure, agent-driven coordination across software and software-defined radio. We are in invitation-only early access and working closely with our first operators.

What we're building

Aithernet brings together an agent runtime, distributed node infrastructure, and secure IP and SDR transports into one platform. The goal is simple: let operators describe what they want done across their software and radio systems, and have it done — securely, observably, and under their control.

Early access

We are onboarding a small number of teams during early access so we can support each one well. If Aithernet fits your work, request access and we'll be in touch.
